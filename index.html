What I do

How I work

Structured, practical, and built for real teams

Most analytics work fails when outputs look polished but do not fit how teams actually operate. I care about the workflow, not just the chart.

  • Start from the decision, not the dashboard
  • Keep definitions documented and easy to reuse
  • Design outputs for the meeting where they will be used
  • Balance speed with enough rigor to trust the result

Typical deliverables

Executive-ready summaries, KPI dashboards, diagnostic analyses, experiment readouts, and operational performance reviews.

Best fit collaborations

Teams that want fewer reporting bottlenecks, better metric hygiene, and decisions grounded in shared evidence.
