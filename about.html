Working style

Principles I use when building ML platforms and teams

  • Production-first engineering: design for reliability, observability, and repeatability from the start.
  • Platform leverage: build reusable tooling and standards that improve throughput across many teams.
  • Clear tradeoffs: document performance, cost, and maintenance decisions so teams can move quickly with confidence.
  • Strong teams compound: mentorship, reviews, and shared standards matter as much as any individual architecture choice.

Where I add value

When a company has strong ML ambition but needs better platform foundations, delivery velocity, or engineering rigor to scale it safely.

What I enjoy most

Turning fragmented model workflows into clean systems that teams can trust, extend, and operate long after the first launch.
